Robyn Rihanna Fenty

The musician turned billionare

Here's a timeline of Rihanna's life:

  • 1988 - Born in Saint Michael, Barbados
  • 2005 - Discovered by American record producer Evan Rogers. The 16-year-old's demo was then sent to Def Jam Recordings, where it was played to the company's new CEO Jay-Z. Jay-Z invited Rihanna to audition and RiRi left the audition with a six album record deal.
  • 2019 - Fenty was a fashion brand by Rihanna under the luxury fashion group LVMH, which launched in May 2019.
  • 2017 - Rihanna launched her critically acclaimed cosmetics company Fenty Beauty under LVMH's Kendo Brands. Rihanna owns 50 percent of Fenty Beauty, according to Forbes.
  • 2022 - The 34-year-old singer recently made Forbes’ annual list of America’s richest self-made women for the third year in a row, ranking 21st overall. She’s the only billionaire under 40 on the list.
